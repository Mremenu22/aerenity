Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 17, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Aerenity ("App"), developed by Sebastian Houng. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our breathing exercise application.

2. Information We Collect

Aerenity is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal information:

  • No Personal Data: We do not require user accounts, and we do not collect personal information such as your name, email address, location, or phone number.
  • Usage Analytics: We collect anonymous usage data to improve the app, such as which breathing exercises you use most frequently and session duration. This data cannot identify you.
  • Device Information: Basic non-identifying device information (OS version, app version) may be collected to ensure compatibility and performance.

3. How We Use Your Information

Any information we collect is used solely to:

  • Improve app functionality and user experience
  • Analyze usage patterns and trends
  • Fix technical issues and bugs
  • Maintain app performance and security

4. Subscription and Payment Information

Important: Aerenity does not handle subscription payments directly. All subscription transactions are managed entirely by Apple through the App Store. Billing information, payment methods, and subscription management are handled by Apple's secure payment infrastructure.

We use RevenueCat for subscription validation and app functionality purposes only. RevenueCat does not receive personal payment or billing information.

5. Third-Party Services

Aerenity uses the following third-party services:

  • RevenueCat: Used for subscription management and entitlement validation. RevenueCat Privacy Policy
  • Apple Services: Payment processing and app distribution. Apple Privacy Policy

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect any data we collect.

7. Children's Privacy

Aerenity is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under 13.

8. Data Retention

We retain anonymous usage analytics for up to 12 months. If you delete the app, no data associated with your device is retained beyond the analytics retention period.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the effective date.
